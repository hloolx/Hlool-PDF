# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Office and operations users who need to stamp, organize, merge, and export PDF documents quickly, often across contract-like document batches where precision and repeatability matter.

## Product Purpose

hlool pdf is a direct-manipulation PDF stamping workspace. It helps users import PDFs or images, place ordinary stamps and seam stamps visually, organize pages, generate stamped outputs, and keep batch workflows fast without destructive edits to source documents.

## Brand Personality

Calm, precise, and quietly polished. The interface should feel like a trustworthy document workbench rather than a decorative file converter.

## Anti-references

Avoid form-heavy panels that make users configure before seeing results, modal-heavy interruption flows, unfinished disabled controls, generic SaaS decoration, and visual changes that compete with document content.

## Design Principles

- Keep document work direct: dragging, selecting, resizing, and previewing should happen where the PDF is visible.
- Make state legible without noise: collapsed panels, selected pages, active tools, and generation progress should be obvious at a glance.
- Preserve trust through precision: sizes, positions, output previews, and generated results should match what users see on the canvas.
- Favor reversible actions over confirmation friction.
- Let the product feel compact and capable before it feels expressive.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve keyboard access for primary workspace actions, keep focus states visible, and respect reduced-motion preferences for theme and panel transitions.
